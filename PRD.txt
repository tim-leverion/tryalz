Tryalz MVP PRD
0. Product Blurb
Tryalz is a workflow system for hospital medical device trials.
It replaces fragmented, inconsistent, and often paper-based trial processes with a centralized system that lets health systems:
* standardize how trials are requested and approved,

* reduce bias in evaluation questionnaires,

* digitize trial feedback collection,

* track trial progress across hospitals,

* close the loop on final product decisions.

The MVP is not a full enterprise platform. It is a lean operating system for one critical workflow:
Sales rep requests trial → value analysis team reviews and governs request/questionnaire → approved trial is launched → nurses submit evaluation feedback digitally → value analysis team reviews results and records final decision.
This matches the main pain points described in the call: siloed trials, lack of corporate oversight, non-standard approvals, biased vendor-provided questionnaires, paper-based data collection, and poor visibility into status and outcomes.   
________________


1. Product Goal
Enable health systems to govern and run medical device trials through one structured workflow, from request to final decision.
The MVP must make these outcomes possible:
   1. Every trial request goes through a standardized approval path.

   2. The value analysis team can review both the trial request and its evaluation questionnaire before the trial starts.

   3. Trial questionnaires are distributed digitally during an active trial.

   4. Nurses can submit evaluations easily without accounts.

   5. Sales reps and value analysis team can monitor trial progress.

   6. The value analysis team can review results and log the final outcome of the trial.

   7. The system becomes the source of truth for historical and active trials.

This is the minimum viable promise of the product: control, visibility, standardization, and reduction of questionnaire bias.   
________________


2. Success Criteria (MVP)
The MVP succeeds if:
      * A sales rep can submit a trial request with required information and questionnaire.

      * A value analysis team can approve, reject, or request edits on the submission.

      * Approved trials can be launched with location, timing, and required evaluation count.

      * Nurses can complete digital evaluations through a simple trial-specific link or QR code.

      * Sales reps can see whether evaluations are being completed.

      * Value analysis team can monitor trial progress and completed evaluations.

      * When the trial ends, the system provides a usable summary of the results.

      * The value analysis team can mark a final status such as approved for adoption or declined.

      * A health system can use the platform as a historical log of trial activity.

Operationally, the MVP should be usable without custom engineering for each trial. The system should support repeated use across multiple hospitals inside one health system.   
________________


3. Product Principles
3.1 Standardization over flexibility
The MVP should enforce a clean process, not attempt to handle every hospital-specific variation.
3.2 Governance before analytics
The first job is controlling trial initiation and questionnaire quality. Fancy reporting is secondary.
3.3 Lean digitization
The MVP does not need complex nurse accounts, deep integrations, or elaborate workflow builders. It needs a working loop.
3.4 Source of truth
A trial must live in one system with a visible status, owners, dates, questionnaire, and final outcome.
3.5 Bias reduction is a core differentiator
Questionnaire review and bias detection are not side features. They are central to the value proposition.

4. Core User Personas
4.1 Sales Rep
External vendor representative proposing a medical device/product for trial.
Primary motivation:
         * get a trial approved,

         * run the trial smoothly,

         * collect evaluations,

         * know final status without chasing people manually.

4.2 Value Analysis Team
Cross-functional decision-making group representing supply chain, clinical leadership, and related stakeholders.
Primary motivation:
            * prevent uncontrolled trials,

            * review and improve trial questionnaires,

            * ensure fair evaluation,

            * monitor progress,

            * make and record adoption decisions.

4.3 Nurse / Clinical Evaluator
Clinical staff member using the product during a trial and submitting feedback.
Primary motivation:
               * complete evaluation quickly with low friction.

4.4 Hospital Sponsor
Internal clinical leader or nurse manager who wants the trial to happen and acts as sponsor/champion for the proposed product.
Primary motivation:
                  * bring a candidate product into the official review process.

This sponsor is operationally important, even if they do not need their own full portal in MVP.

6. Core User Journeys
6.1 Sales Rep Journey
                                                                     1. Pitch product to clinical stakeholders at a hospital.

                                                                     2. Gain informal interest and identify hospital sponsor.

                                                                     3. Submit trial request in Tryalz.

                                                                     4. Attach proposed evaluation questionnaire.

                                                                     5. Wait for value analysis team review.

                                                                     6. Receive one of three outcomes:

                                                                        * approved,

                                                                        * changes requested,

                                                                        * rejected.

                                                                           7. If changes requested, edit and resubmit.

                                                                           8. If approved, view trial details:

                                                                              * date,

                                                                              * location,

                                                                              * duration,

                                                                              * required number of evaluations.

                                                                                 9. Access trial-specific QR code/link.

                                                                                 10. Share QR code/link during the active trial.

                                                                                 11. Monitor evaluation completion/progress.

                                                                                 12. After trial closes, see final decision status without manual chasing.

6.2 Value Analysis Team Journey
                                                                                    1. Open new trial requests queue.

                                                                                    2. Review request details:

                                                                                       * product,

                                                                                       * sponsor,

                                                                                       * proposed locations,

                                                                                       * questionnaire.

                                                                                          3. Review AI bias/quality analysis of questionnaire.

                                                                                          4. Approve, reject, or request edits.

                                                                                          5. If approved, define operational setup:

                                                                                             * hospitals/units,

                                                                                             * dates,

                                                                                             * duration,

                                                                                             * evaluation count.

                                                                                                6. Monitor trial progress while active.

                                                                                                7. Review trial summary/report after completion.

                                                                                                8. Meet offline if needed for internal decision-making.

                                                                                                9. Record final decision in system.

                                                                                                10. Maintain historical visibility of past trials.

6.3 Nurse / Evaluator Journey
                                                                                                   1. Receive QR code or link from sales rep or on-site process.

                                                                                                   2. Open questionnaire on mobile or web.

                                                                                                   3. Complete evaluation.

                                                                                                   4. Submit response.

                                                                                                   5. Exit. No login required.

This is the correct lean flow. Nurses do not need dashboards or accounts in MVP. The hospital sponsor does not need a heavy workflow either. Most governance sits with sales rep and value analysis team.

7. Functional Requirements
7.1 Authentication and Roles
Must-have
                                                                                                      * User login for sales reps and value analysis team.

                                                                                                      * Role-based access control.

                                                                                                      * Health-system-level data partitioning.

                                                                                                      * Sales reps can only access their own trial requests/trials unless explicitly expanded later.

                                                                                                      * Value analysis team can access all relevant trial activity for their health system.

Out of scope
                                                                                                         * SSO

                                                                                                         * complex permission matrices

                                                                                                         * nurse authentication

________________


7.2 Trial Request Submission
Must-have
Sales rep can create and submit a trial request with:
                                                                                                            * product/device name

                                                                                                            * vendor/manufacturer name

                                                                                                            * sponsor name and role

                                                                                                            * target health system

                                                                                                            * target hospital(s) or intended location(s)

                                                                                                            * notes/context

                                                                                                            * requested dates or timeframe

                                                                                                            * requested trial duration

                                                                                                            * requested evaluation count

                                                                                                            * questionnaire attachment or direct questionnaire builder input

Behaviors
                                                                                                               * Request can be saved as draft before submission.

                                                                                                               * Once submitted, status becomes submitted.

                                                                                                               * Value analysis team is notified.

                                                                                                               * Questionnaire is treated as part of the request, not a separate later artifact.

Constraints
                                                                                                                  * No advanced workflow designer needed.

                                                                                                                  * A simple form with structured fields is sufficient.

This is critical because the current real-world problem is that requests are inconsistent, informal, and often not governed centrally.   
________________


7.3 Questionnaire Intake and Review
Must-have
                                                                                                                     * Sales rep can submit evaluation questions as part of the trial request.

                                                                                                                     * Value analysis team can review questionnaire before approval.

                                                                                                                     * Each questionnaire has a visible version attached to the request.

                                                                                                                     * Reviewer can request edits to specific questions.

AI-assisted review
The system should run a lightweight automated review on submitted questions and produce:
                                                                                                                        * possible bias flags,

                                                                                                                        * problematic phrasing flags,

                                                                                                                        * leading question flags,

                                                                                                                        * imbalance flags,

                                                                                                                        * suggested edits or caution notes.

Reviewer actions
                                                                                                                           * approve questionnaire as-is,

                                                                                                                           * request changes,

                                                                                                                           * reject request entirely,

                                                                                                                           * add comments tied to question numbers or general comments.

Constraints
                                                                                                                              * AI review is advisory, not autonomous.

                                                                                                                              * AI does not block submission on its own.

                                                                                                                              * Final decision remains with humans.

This is one of the clearest differentiators from current alternatives and one of the strongest reasons a value analysis team would care.   
________________


7.4 Trial Request Review Workflow
Must-have
Value analysis team can view a queue of trial requests with:
                                                                                                                                 * status

                                                                                                                                 * submitting sales rep

                                                                                                                                 * sponsor

                                                                                                                                 * product name

                                                                                                                                 * target hospitals

                                                                                                                                 * submission date

Each request detail page must show:
                                                                                                                                    * full request details

                                                                                                                                    * questionnaire

                                                                                                                                    * AI review output

                                                                                                                                    * comment history

                                                                                                                                    * action buttons

Available actions
                                                                                                                                       * Approve

                                                                                                                                       * Request Changes

                                                                                                                                       * Reject

On Request Changes
                                                                                                                                          * reviewer enters comments,

                                                                                                                                          * system updates status to changes_requested,

                                                                                                                                          * sales rep is notified,

                                                                                                                                          * sales rep can edit and resubmit.

On Reject
                                                                                                                                             * reviewer enters reason,

                                                                                                                                             * system updates status to rejected.

On Approve
                                                                                                                                                * request progresses to trial setup.

________________


7.5 Trial Setup After Approval
Must-have
On approval, the value analysis team must define:
                                                                                                                                                   * trial start date,

                                                                                                                                                   * trial end date or duration,

                                                                                                                                                   * participating hospital(s),

                                                                                                                                                   * unit/department if relevant,

                                                                                                                                                   * required number of evaluations/forms,

                                                                                                                                                   * final approved questionnaire version.

The system then creates an active Trial record.
Outputs
                                                                                                                                                      * unique trial page

                                                                                                                                                      * unique trial QR code or shareable link

                                                                                                                                                      * visible status: scheduled or active

Constraints
                                                                                                                                                         * No calendar sync required for MVP.

                                                                                                                                                         * No hospital-side workflow automation required.

8. End-to-End Process Definition
This is the operating loop another AI or builder must preserve.
Phase 1: Opportunity formation
Outside the system:
                                                                                                                                                                                                         * sales rep pitches product,

                                                                                                                                                                                                         * hospital-side sponsor expresses interest.

Phase 2: Trial request governance
Inside Tryalz:
                                                                                                                                                                                                            * sales rep submits request and questionnaire,

                                                                                                                                                                                                            * value analysis team reviews,

                                                                                                                                                                                                            * AI assists with question quality review,

                                                                                                                                                                                                            * request is approved, rejected, or sent back for edits.

Phase 3: Trial launch
Inside Tryalz:
                                                                                                                                                                                                               * value analysis team sets dates, locations, and evaluation requirements,

                                                                                                                                                                                                               * system creates active trial and QR/link.

Phase 4: Trial execution
Partly outside, partly inside:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * sales rep shares QR/link during trial,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * nurses submit evaluations digitally,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * sales rep and value analysis team monitor progress.

Phase 5: Trial closure and decision
Inside Tryalz:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * system summarizes results,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * value analysis team records final decision,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * sales rep sees outcome,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * historical record remains stored.

That is the essential product loop. Anything outside it is secondary.

11. Assumptions and Constraints
Assumptions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * Hospitals/value analysis teams are willing to use a centralized approval workflow.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * Sales reps are willing to submit requests formally if that is the required path.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * Nurses will complete mobile/web forms if friction is low.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * One questionnaire per trial is sufficient for MVP, even if later there may be variants by department or hospital.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * Value analysis team decision-making may still happen partly offline; the system only needs to capture the result.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 * AI questionnaire review is positioned as an assistive layer, not a regulatory-grade validator.

Constraints
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * Real hospital processes vary widely.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * The first version should optimize for demonstrability and utility, not perfect configurability.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * Early customers may use the system as a guided process rather than fully self-serve software.

These constraints follow directly from the call: the product is still being shaped around live buyer feedback, and the immediate need is a strong, coherent demoable workflow.

14. Key Product Risks
14.1 Process variation
Each health system may run value analysis differently. MVP must avoid overfitting to one exact org chart.
14.2 Bias review trust
If AI review is weak or noisy, it undermines the differentiation. Position it carefully as assistant, not authority.
14.3 Submission friction
If sales reps find request submission too heavy, adoption may suffer.
14.4 Nurse completion rate
If the evaluation form is clunky, trial data quality collapses.
14.5 Decision-making remains offline
The product may support workflow but not fully own the real decision process. The MVP must still capture closure cleanly.

16. One-Sentence Product Definition for Builders
Tryalz MVP is a B2B workflow app that standardizes medical device trials for health systems by centralizing trial approval, questionnaire governance, digital evaluation collection, progress tracking, and final decision logging. 
17. Builder Notes for Another AI
When interpreting this PRD, preserve these truths:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               * The real buyer is the health system/value analysis function, not the sales rep.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               * The sales rep is a core actor, but not the owner of governance.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               * Bias reduction in questionnaires is a major differentiator.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               * Trial request governance and trial execution are distinct but connected workflows.
